Generica Smoothie Recipe

Description

    Ingredients

  • 1 cup liquid, juice, milk, or coconut water, add more as needed
  • Â¼ cup yogurt, Greek, regular, dairy-free, plain or unsweetened
  • 1 cup fresh fruit or vegetables
  • 1 cup leafy greens
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 2 cups frozen fruit
  • 1 cup ice cubes, add more as needed

      • Steps

    1. Add liquid, yogurt (if using), fresh fruit/vegetables, leafy greens, frozen fruits, ice cubes, and sweetener (if using) to the blender.
    2. Cover and process on medium speed for about 30 seconds, using the tamper as needed
    3. Increase speed to high for 15 to 30 seconds until the smoothie is thick and smooth. Add more ice or liquid as needed to achieve the desired consistency.
